Game Instructions

Objective: Remember the numbers in the grid and recall them in the correct order.

How to Play:

  • Select a level from the settings menu.
  • Choose a timer duration from the settings menu.
  • Click the "Play" button to start the game.
  • Memorize the grid of numbers for the duration of the timer.
  • When the timer expires, try to recall the numbers in the correct order by dragging and dropping them into the grid.
  • Click the "Check Answer" button to see how well you did.
